Tajine

Description

A classic Moroccan tajine with warm spices and tender ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Chicken (or vegetables)
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Spices (ginger, turmeric, cumin)
  • Preserved lemon / olives (optional)

Steps

  1. Prep and season ingredients.
  2. Sauté onions, add protein/veg and spices.
  3. Simmer until tender.
  4. Serve warm.
