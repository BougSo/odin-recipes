Harira

Description

A comforting Moroccan soup with tomatoes, lentils, and herbs.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Lentils
  • Chickpeas
  • Celery + herbs
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Sauté aromatics and spices.
  2. Add tomatoes, lentils, chickpeas, and simmer.
  3. Adjust seasoning and serve.
